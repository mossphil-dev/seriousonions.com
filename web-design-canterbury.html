Web Design Services in Canterbury

Canterbury is one of the busiest cities in Kent — and one of the most competitive places to run a business. Between the tourists drawn by the cathedral and the World Heritage Site, the student population from UCA, Canterbury Christ Church and the University of Kent, and a high street that fights hard to hold its own against online giants, there's a constantly shifting mix of people looking for local businesses to spend their money with. The question is whether they can find you.

The hospitality sector in Canterbury is huge. Restaurants, cafés, bars and hotels compete for the same footfall every single week. A well-designed website with good photography, clear menus, easy booking links and strong local SEO is no longer optional — it's table stakes. The same goes for retail and services: if your site looks like it was built in 2012 and takes ten seconds to load on a phone, students and tourists won't wait around. They'll find someone else.
