Treehouse · Sleeps 2 · Cottenham, Cambridgeshire

Orchards Nook Treehouse

📍 9 miles from Cambridge city centre

A unique and lovingly built treehouse nestled within a small private orchard on a picturesque 10-acre plot. Designed around an actual tree, it combines rustic charm with modern comforts — a hand-built king-size bed, modern shower room, and kitchenette inside, with a decked garden area and a secluded rooftop space complete with outdoor bath and seating outside.

The grounds are home to four resident horses, two friendly dogs, and a wealth of wildlife — hares, deer, badgers, and red kites overhead. Guests are welcome to greet the horses and treat them to a carrot.

Sleeps 2 · Adults only · No pets

Barn Retreat · Sleeps 2 · Cottenham, Cambridgeshire

The Cosy Hide

📍 15–20 minutes from Cambridge · 15 mins walk to Great River Ouse

A caravan with a big difference — set inside a barn with the most spectacular sunset views across open Cambridgeshire countryside. It has a generous inside/outside area, perfect for dining and relaxing, plus a large outdoor space complete with a fire pit and grill. Truly one of a kind.

Cottage · Sleeps 4 · Cottenham, Cambridgeshire

Cambridgeshire Cosy Cottage

📍 9 miles from Cambridge · Close to Ely · Great River Ouse nearby

Nestled on a picturesque 10-acre plot in rural Cottenham, this beautifully equipped cottage is the perfect escape for those wanting genuine countryside peace within easy reach of Cambridge.

Upstairs there are two roomy bedrooms — a twin and a king with a hand-crafted bed — plus an upstairs bathroom with a roll-top bath and separate shower. Downstairs, the fully equipped kitchen features an Aga stove, a separate lounge with wood burner, and French doors onto a private garden.

Cottage · Sleeps 4 · Barham, Kent

Kent Cosy Cottage

📍 Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty · Canterbury 7 miles · Dover 14 miles

Set on the outskirts of the picturesque village of Barham within an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, this delightful home offers breath-taking, far-reaching views across miles of stunning countryside.

Arranged over three floors, the top floor has a double bedroom with en-suite shower room and Velux views; the middle floor has a king bedroom next to the modern bathroom. Downstairs, a cosy living room with open fire and kitchen/diner with bifold doors opening onto a balcony.
