Sitra Ale All-Grain Recipe (23 Liters) Method: All Grain Style: American Pale Ale / English IPA Batch Size: 23 Liters Original Gravity (OG): ~1.049–1.050 Final Gravity (FG): ~1.008–1.012 ABV: ~4.5% – 5.3% IBU: ~46–59 (High bitterness) Ingredients Fermentables 4.3 kg — Maris Otter Pale Malt (Approx. 91%) 300 g — Munich Malt (Approx. 6%) 125 g — Cara Malt (Approx. 3%) Hops 20 g — Citra (Boil 70 min) 10 g — Citra (Boil 15 min) 25 g — Simcoe (Boil 15 min) Optional: Additional Citra/Simcoe for dry hopping after fermentation. Yeast Fermentis SafAle US-05 (Dry) or similar clean American Ale yeast. Other 5 g — Irish Moss (Boil 15 min) Brewing Method Mash: Mash at 65°C–67°C for 90 minutes. Boil: Boil for 60-70 minutes, adding hops as scheduled above. Fermentation: Ferment at 19°C–20°C using US-05 yeast. Dry Hop: Add dry hops to the fermenter 3-5 days before bottling/kegging. Note: This recipe is commonly found on Brewer's Friend, often listed as "Sitra Recipe Pack" by BrewUK.
